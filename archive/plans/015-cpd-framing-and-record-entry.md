# CPD Framing And Record Entry

## Summary

Extend the landing and learn experience so Calibrate is presented as a place
for continuous professional development, with recording events introduced as a
first-class product path alongside reflection and assessment.

## Decisions

- Keep the landing page sparse and keep `Let's calibrate!` as the primary CTA.
- Add explicit CPD wording to the landing page.
- Keep `simple`, `personal`, and `local-first` as links into `/learn`.
- Link `record events` to `/record`.
- Link `self-reflect or self-assess` to `/start`.
- Add a plain-language `recording events` section to `/learn`.
- Introduce `/record` as an intentional thin entry page for now.

## Deliverables

- updated landing-page copy and inline links
- expanded `/learn` content covering event recording
- new `/record` route and placeholder page

## Acceptance Criteria

- the landing page stays calm and low-density
- CPD is explicit on the landing page
- `record events` routes to `/record`
- `self-reflect or self-assess` routes to `/start`
- `/learn` explains recording events alongside reflection and assessment
